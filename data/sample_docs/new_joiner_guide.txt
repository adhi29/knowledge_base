WELCOME TO CITI BANKING OPERATIONS
==============================================================================

This guide covers everything you need to know in your first 90 days as a new
Operations team member. Save this document and refer back to it whenever you
need a reminder on systems, processes, or who to contact.

==============================================================================
WEEK 1: GETTING STARTED
==============================================================================

Day 1 Checklist:
[ ] Collect badge and hardware from IT (Room 4B, Level 2)
[ ] Set up SSO (Single Sign-On) with your employee ID — IT Help Desk ext. 5555
[ ] Complete mandatory security awareness training (link in welcome email)
[ ] Review Code of Conduct and sign acknowledgement form
[ ] Meet your buddy (assigned by your manager) for a systems walk-through

Key Systems You Will Access:
1. Flexcube — Core Banking System (request access via ServiceNow ticket)
2. Actimize — AML/Transaction Monitoring (Compliance team only)
3. SharePoint — Document repository (auto-provisioned)
4. Confluence — Team wiki and SOPs
5. ServiceNow — IT ticketing system (self-service at servicenow.citi.internal)
6. Outlook — Email (provisioned on Day 1)

==============================================================================
SYSTEM ACCESS GUIDE
==============================================================================

Flexcube Access:
- Submit ServiceNow request: Category = "Core Banking", Type = "New User Access"
- Requires manager approval + IT Security sign-off (SLA: 3 business days)
- Mandatory training: Flexcube Fundamentals (2 hours, available on Citi Learn)
- Temporary access denied: Contact ops-support@citi.com

Actimize Access (Compliance team only):
- Submit request via: compliance-access@citi.com
- Requires BSA/AML certification (complete before requesting)
- SLA: 5 business days

SWIFT/GFTS Access (for wire transfer processing):
- Available to authorized WTOs (Wire Transfer Operators) only
- Requires completion of Wire Transfer SOP training (SOP-OPS-001)
- Dual-factor authentication mandatory

==============================================================================
ESCALATION PATHS
==============================================================================

If you are unsure about a transaction or process, follow this escalation path:

Level 1: Your direct Team Lead (for day-to-day operational questions)
Level 2: Operations Manager (for exceptions and policy interpretation)
Level 3: Compliance Team (for regulatory questions, SAR, CTR)
Level 4: Legal / Risk (for unusual regulatory requests)

Never attempt to resolve a compliance issue independently — always escalate.

TRAINING PLAN — FIRST 90 DAYS
==============================================================================

Week 1-2:
- Security awareness training (mandatory, Day 1)
- Flexcube Fundamentals (Citi Learn, 2 hours)
- Banking Operations Overview (Citi Learn, 3 hours)
- Shadow your buddy for live transaction processing

Week 3-4:
- Wire Transfer Processing (SOP-OPS-001 + practical assessment)
- KYC Fundamentals (mandatory for all, 4 hours on Citi Learn)
- OFAC Screening practical exercise with Team Lead

Week 5-8:
- Transaction Monitoring Basics (if applicable to your role)
- Product-specific training (assigned by manager)
- First independent transaction processing (supervised)

Week 9-12:
- First performance check-in with manager
- Compliance certification exams (if required for your role)
- Full independent processing with periodic review

==============================================================================
FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
==============================================================================

Q: How long does Flexcube access take?
A: Typically 3 business days after your ServiceNow request is approved by your manager.

Q: Can I process wire transfers on my first week?
A: No. You must complete Wire Transfer SOP training and pass the practical assessment first.
   Most new joiners begin independent wire processing in weeks 5-8.

Q: What do I do if I accidentally initiate a wrong wire?
A: Do NOT try to fix it yourself. Immediately contact your Team Lead and the Operations
   Manager. Time is critical — some wires can be recalled if caught within 30 minutes.

Q: Who approves my leave?
A: Your direct line manager via Workday (the HR system). Submit requests at least 2 weeks
   in advance for planned leave.

Q: I spotted something that looks suspicious in a transaction. What do I do?
A: Do not take any action on the transaction. Flag it to your Team Lead immediately
   and do NOT inform the customer. Your Team Lead will escalate to Compliance if needed.

Q: Where are the SOPs stored?
A: All SOPs are on Confluence under Operations > Standard Operating Procedures.
   Policy documents are on SharePoint under Compliance > Policies.
